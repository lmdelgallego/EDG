Help

FAQ'S

What is Contagious Ideas?

Contagious Ideas is a forum for you to share your creativity and win accolades as well as cash awards. Will your entry go viral? Enter contests and leverage social media to get your work seen by the world.

How long do contests take, and what's involved?

Contests vary according to type, genre, areas of interest, as well as what is asked of you, the entrant. The duration of the contest generally reflects the amount of time and effort expected

When I win, how do I claim my prize?

If you win you will receive an emailed notification. Just click on the link provided, provide the requisite information, and we’ll do the rest!

How I sponsor a contest?

Contact us at support@contagiousideas.com and we’ll help you get that in motion.

If my entry has the most up-votes in a contest am I the winner?

No, having the most up-votes does not mean you have won the contest. It does however ensure increased visibility.

Who decides the individual contests' winner?

The contests’ sponsors determine judges/judging for each individual contest.

Can I edit my entry after it's been published?

Yes, your entry can be edited, but resubmitting the same entry may erase your accumulated Upvotes. Proofread and verify your Entry before submitting (maybe even twice) for better results.

Are there ever ties?

If a sponsor deems multiple entries are of the highest quality that is at their sole discretion. Please review out Terms of Service for complete details.

How many times can I enter the same contest?

The number of times you can enter a contest varies from contest to contest. Click on the “contest details” button on any individual contest’s page and you’ll find your answer.

What are the benefits of becoming a premium member?

As a premium member you are eligible to enter an unlimited number of contests. You will also be eligible to enter select premium contests for the chance to win larger cash prizes.
Premium membership is awesome and for only $10/month you’ll agree it’s worth every penny.

How is Premium Membership billed?

User choice, monthly or annually; and of course, you can modify your selection at any time. We want to be treated like you want to be treated! We’re with you like that.

What happens to my entries into premium contests if I go back to being a free member?

Your entries will remain in all contests previously entered, even those that have not yet closed.

How is copyright handled?

Members own the copyright to their entries until they are declared a contest winner by the contest’s sponsor, and the rights to the winning entry are transferred over to the contest holder.

Do I need to have existing student loans to enter a contest? To win?

No. All you need to enter and win is an education.

What does "original work" mean?

You have to outright own what you enter into contests. No using other people’s work. Also, the entry cannot be part of another contest with a possible prize of over $250.
